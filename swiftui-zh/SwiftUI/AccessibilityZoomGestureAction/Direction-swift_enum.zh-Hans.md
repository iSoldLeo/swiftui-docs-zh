# AccessibilityZoomGestureAction.Direction

**Enumeration**

A direction that matches the movement of a zoom gesture performed by an assistive technology, such as a swipe up and down in Voiceover’s zoom rotor.

## Declaration

```swift
@frozen enum Direction
```

## Getting the direction

- **AccessibilityZoomGestureAction.Direction.zoomIn**: The gesture direction that represents zooming in.
- **AccessibilityZoomGestureAction.Direction.zoomOut**: The gesture direction that represents zooming out.

## Getting the action’s direction

- **direction**: The zoom gesture’s direction.
