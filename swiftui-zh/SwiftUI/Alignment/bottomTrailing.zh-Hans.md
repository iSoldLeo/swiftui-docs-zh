# bottomTrailing

**Type Property**

A guide that marks the bottom and trailing edges of the view.

## Declaration

```swift
static let bottomTrailing: Alignment
```

## Discussion

This alignment combines the [trailing](../HorizontalAlignment/trailing.zh-Hans.md) horizontal guide and the [bottom](../VerticalAlignment/bottom.zh-Hans.md) vertical guide:



## Getting bottom guides

- **bottomLeading**: A guide that marks the bottom and leading edges of the view.
- **bottom**: A guide that marks the bottom edge of the view.
