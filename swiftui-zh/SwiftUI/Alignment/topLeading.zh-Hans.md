# topLeading

**Type Property**

A guide that marks the top and leading edges of the view.

## Declaration

```swift
static let topLeading: Alignment
```

## Discussion

This alignment combines the [leading](../HorizontalAlignment/leading.zh-Hans.md) horizontal guide and the [top](../VerticalAlignment/top.zh-Hans.md) vertical guide:



## Getting top guides

- **top**: A guide that marks the top edge of the view.
- **topTrailing**: A guide that marks the top and trailing edges of the view.
