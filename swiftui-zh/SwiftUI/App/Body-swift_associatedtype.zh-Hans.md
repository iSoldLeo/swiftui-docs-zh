# Body

**Associated Type**

The type of scene representing the content of the app.

## Declaration

```swift
associatedtype Body : Scene
```

## Discussion

When you create a custom app, Swift infers this type from your implementation of the required [body-swift.property](body-swift_property.zh-Hans.md) property.

## Implementing an app

- **body**: The content and behavior of the app.
