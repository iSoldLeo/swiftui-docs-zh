# MagnificationGesture

**Structure**

A gesture that recognizes a magnification motion and tracks the amount of magnification.

## Declaration

```swift
struct MagnificationGesture
```

## Creating the gesture

- **init(minimumScaleDelta:)**: Creates a magnification gesture with a given minimum delta for the gesture to start.
- **minimumScaleDelta**: The minimum required delta before the gesture starts.

## Deprecated gestures

- **RotationGesture**: A gesture that recognizes a rotation motion and tracks the angle of the rotation.

## Conforms To

- Gesture
