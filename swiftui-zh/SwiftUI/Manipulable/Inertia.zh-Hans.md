# Manipulable.Inertia

**Structure**

Describes inertia of a view that defines how much a view resists being manipulated.

## Declaration

```swift
struct Inertia
```

## Type Properties

- **high**: A view has high inertia and high resistance to being manipulated.
- **low**: A view has low inertia and low resistance to being manipulated.
- **medium**: A view has medium inertia and medium resistance to being manipulated.
- **none**: A view has no inertia and no resistance to being manipulated.
