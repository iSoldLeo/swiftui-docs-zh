**Enumeration**

```swift

```

- **headerProminence(_:)**：设置此视图的标题显著性。

- **headerProminence**：应用于视图内节标题的显著性。

# Prominence

**Enumeration**

A type indicating the prominence of a view hierarchy.

## Declaration

```swift
enum Prominence
```

## Getting prominence options

- **Prominence.standard**: The standard prominence.
- **Prominence.increased**: An increased prominence.

## Configuring headers

- **headerProminence(_:)**: Sets the header prominence for this view.
- **headerProminence**: The prominence to apply to section headers within a view.

## Conforms To

- Copyable
- Equatable
- Hashable
- Sendable
- SendableMetatype
