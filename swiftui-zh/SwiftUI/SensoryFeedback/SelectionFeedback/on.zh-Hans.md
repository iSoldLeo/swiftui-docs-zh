**类型属性**

# on

**Type Property**

Indicates that a control’s value has changed to on, such as when a toggle is tapped to turn on.

## Declaration

```swift
static let on: SensoryFeedback.SelectionFeedback
```

## Discussion

Only plays feedback on visionOS.
